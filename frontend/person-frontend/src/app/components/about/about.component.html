ABOUT US

I was born in good ol’ Madison, Wisconsin. The son of a Librarian and a Researcher. We moved to Miami, FL in 1992 and experienced our first foray in the world of tropical weather: Hurricane Andrew. We survived that night, a sister was born that night, and I continued to grow, with tons of other stuff that happened. Many years later I attended the University of Florida, intent on studying biotechnology or something marine science-y. Instead, I swapped majors and graduated with a BFA in graphic design, because that’s what you do when you don’t want to do science and maths or learn no more. I continued to learn things though.
